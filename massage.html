Holistic massage

Over the centuries and across the world, the effect of touch has proved effective in curing ailments in the body. Employing a variety of techniques, holistic massage relaxes muscles and stimulates the nervous and lymphatic system. This releases the oxygen in the blood and helps the body eliminate toxins and metabolic waste. Massage can help the body heal itself, reduce recovery time after an injury, boost the immune system and increase vitality.

In addition to medical benefits, holistic massage improves general health and wellbeing by relaxing the whole body and mind.

Treatment sessions

Each treatment session starts with a small consultation to assess what area needs to be focused on, as well as identify any possible contraindications. This is important to ensure the session is safe and effective. After the consultation, you will receive the treatment most suitable to your needs, incorporating the techniques from Swedish Massage, Indian Head Massage* and Reiki*.

*It is also possible to receive Indian Head Massage alone or Reiki alone, please contact for further information.

Who benefits from treatment?

The treatment is tailored to your individual needs. It may benefit many conditions and ailments including:

• Physical discomfort/pain
• Stress relief
• Head/neck/shoulder stiffness & pain
• Back pain
• Injury recovery
• Migraine & Headache
• Insomnia
• Well being maintenance before / during / after pregnancy
• …And improving general health and wellbeing

Of course you do not need to have particular symptoms or conditions. You can simply relax and have a moment to yourself. …!
